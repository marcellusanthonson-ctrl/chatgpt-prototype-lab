# PAT-LAB-001 — Record events, not full transcripts

Status: `VALIDATED`
Scope: `LAB`, `MammothSkills`, `Symphonie`
Updated-At: `2026-06-28`

## Intent

Preserve durable learning without copying complete conversations.

## Procedure

1. Identify a material proposal, error, decision, experiment, or state change.
2. Record context, impact, evidence, outcome, and reusable rule.
3. Link the canonical artifact and omit conversational detail that adds no
   audit value.

## Anti-patterns

- saving full transcripts by default;
- omitting evidence or lifecycle state;
- treating a summary as new approval.
